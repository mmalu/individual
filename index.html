Stories

The hustle to TA two classes at CMU
The notion at CMU was that you can only TA one class on a student visa. An international student could only work part-time up to 20 hours and thus could TA only one class. Having learned so much at CMU, I was determined to give back. I reached out to two professors saying I’ll do the work for 20 hours but only bill you for 10 as I want to TA two classes. The hard part was to make them trust that I wouldn't bill them 20 hours each and get into visa trouble. I made sure to meet both of them in person to convince that I don’t care about the money part.

Computer Science
It was love in first sight when I took the data structures class in my sophomore year. IOn called Malegaon was so obsessed that I used to study and do assignments that were scheduled for future classes. This obsession brought took me to CMU. However, I flunked a test in the algorithm class which made me realize how little I knew about CS. The following summer, I worked my way up starting from freshman classes to master classes - one brick at a time.

On called Malegaon
Malegaon is the town I belong to. I moved to Pune to study engineering. All the “city” guys there used to refer to me as Malegaon. I used to feel good about it at first. I did not see the contempt in it. It took a while to realize it and once it did, oh boy it hurt bad. In retrospect, it seems so stupid of me that I let myself be defined based on what they thought of me.

A lesson in not to steal
Once a cashier at a restaurant tendered more change to us. I gleefully grabbed it and let my uncle know that we made money. He right away snapped & went back to return the money. When he returned the money, the look on the cashier's face look said it loud how much that money meant to him. That look of gratitude and respect inspired me not to steal again.
